    The samples emails have been anonymized and any names used are made up and not intended to represent real people. The attack emails are representative of attackers, not any company that may be impersonated.
Here are a few sample conversations between a human and bot based on ask detection module of our Panacea system.

Conversation Sample 1

Agent:

Dear Colleagues,
I don't know all of you but I'm a JPL employee in the robotics group. I am participating in the 2019 MORE THAN PINK Walk on September 22nd to raise money in the fight against breast cancer. Like many of you, this disease has struck close to home. Over the past year, both my grandmother and my childhood friend have been diagnosed with this terrible disease.

And so I'm asking for donations. I'm not used to, and frankly hate, asking for money, but I know this is not for me. This is for all of the women in our lives and ALL donations will help fund the research and treatment of breast cancer.

My goal is to raise $3,000. You can help! Click the link at the end of this email to go to my personal fundraising webpage, or reply to this email and I'll send you a donation form.

Thank you, thank you, thank you!
Emanuela Palombo

Bot:

Hi Emanuela,
Thanks for your email. I tried clicking on the link. The website is not opening for me. I tried out different browsers. Did you provie me with the right link? Is this the one?
Thanks
